Fullmetal Alchemist Comics

About

Edward and Alphonse Elric are two gifted alchemist brothers, but their disregard for alchemy's fundamental laws caused them gravely.

When they tried to revive their mother though alchemy, something went terribly wrong. Edward lost his leg and arms while Alphonse, his entire body and his spirit has been placed in a metal suit of armor. Now on a mission to recover their bodies, the two sets out for the Philosopher's Stone, a legendary catalyst and a powerful object that can help them restore their original forms.

This Japanese comic book series was created by Hiromu Arakawa. A sci fi fantasy manga that is styled after European Industrial Revolution. Fullmetal Alchemist is a new comics that was first released on on 2001 and published by Enix.
